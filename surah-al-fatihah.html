Surah Al Fatihah

Our Messenger was overwhelmed with opposition, and in the midst of that opposition Allah gave him something to be optimistic about. Allah says We have given you the seven oft repeated ones that are full of praise and additionally We have given you the Great Quran. These seven oft repeated ones full of praise are actually the Fatihah. Allah is telling us I gave you the Fatihah and then I gave you the Quran. We repeat the Fatihah more than anything else. Everytime we stand in prayer we recite the Fatihah. Every opportunity we get we recite the Fatihah. We recite the opening of the Fatihah, Alhamdulillah, all the time. This part of the Quran Allah inspired us to repeat more than any other part because it shapes our view for the rest of the Quran. This is why the Fatihah is placed at the beginning of the Quran. If anyone wants to embark on a journey to start the Quran, there is no better place to start than the Fatihah. How Allah separates the Fatihah from the rest of the Quran is absolutely remarkable. It stands in its place all on its own. So I invite you to this first step of the Journey of the Quran, The Fatihah.
