Surah Al Fatihah: Conclusion

Praise and Thanks

Without praise then thanks is missing. Without thanks then praise is is missing. Hamd balances it.

Ar Rahman

If you just say Ar Rahman and Ar Raheem then someone could take advantage of the love and care of Allah. Malik Yom Ad Deen balances it and displays the justice of Allah.

Nouns and Verbs

The first three ayat of the Fatihah is about Allah and it is a noun based sentence because Allah is timeless. The middle ayah of the fatihah is about us and Allah and it is a verb and noun sentence. The last three ayah of the Fatihah is about us and it is a verb based sentence because we are temporary.

Intro and Conclusion

The first three ayat of the fatihah is about Allah and the first half of the middle ayah concludes the statement. The last three ayat are about us and the second half of the middle ayah introduces it.

Knowledge and Action

The first three ayat of the fatihah is about knowledge. The middle ayah of the fatihah is about action. The last three ayat of the fatihah is about knowledge and action.

The Three Kinds of People

  1. Those whom Allah has favored
  2. Those who had wrath upon them
  3. Those who are lost

The Conflicts in Society

  1. The Social Conflict - The Conflict Between Men and Women
    • Mens will favor men
    • Women will favor women
    • It is because they are biased

  2. The Economic Conflict - The Conflict Between Employer and Employee
    • Employer says more work with less pay
    • Employee says less work and more pay
    • Employer says less vacation and employee says more vacation

  3. The Political Conflict - The Conflict Between Government and People
    • Governments want more control over the people
    • Government want more taxes from the people
    • People want less control from the government
    • People want to pay less taxes
    • If you let people decide there will be lawlessness and chaos
    • If you let governments decide there will be anarchy

  4. The Internal Conflict - The Conflict Between Body and Soul
    • Body says more exercise and less sleep
    • Soul says more sleep
    • If you listen to the body then the soul dies
    • If you listen to the soul then the body dies

Allah is above the creation and not associated with any of the above parties. No one can resolve the above issues with justice except for Allah. This is the guidance to the straight path. Unless we go back to Allah the conflict will continue. There will be no peace or harmony without Allah. So who will be the judge who will bring balance and justice into this world.
